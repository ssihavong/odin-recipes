Panda Express Orange Chicken

Description

Panda Express Orange Chicken is a guilty pleasure of mine. It is my favorite go-to when I'm craving something quick.

You can never go wrong whether it's at an airport or at a random strip mall.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless skinless chicken chopped into bit sixed pieces
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • white pepper
  • oil (for frying)
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup flour

    • Orange Sauce

  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 3/4 cup orange juice
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 orange, zest of
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 2 tablespoons gingerroot, minced
  • 2 teaspoons garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon red chili pepper flakes
  • 1/2 cup green onion
  • 2 tablespoons rice wine
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Steps

  1. Place chicken pieces in a large bowl and set aside
  2. In a medium bowl, stir egg, salt, pepper and 1 tablespoon oil and mix well, set aside.
  3. In a medium bowl, stir together 1.2 cup of the cornstarch and flour.
  4. Heat oil for deep frying in wok or deep-fryer to 375 degrees.
  5. Dip chicken pieces into egg mixture, then into the flour mixture, shaking off any excess.
  6. Add chickenb to wok in small batches and fry for 3-4 minutes or until golder brown.
  7. (Do not overcook chicken)
  8. Remove chicken from oil with slotted spoona nd drain on paper towels; set aside.

    9. For the sauce:

  9. In a small bowl, combine the soy sauce, orange juice, brown sugar, and orange zest. Set aside.
  10. Clean wok and heat 15 seconds over high heat.
  11. Add 1 tablespoon oil, the gingerroot, garlice, crushed red chili pepper flakes, and green onions and sitr fry for just a few seconds until fragrant.
  12. Now, add the rice wine and the soy sauce mixture you set aside above.
  13. Add cooked chicken, stirring until well mixed.
  14. In a small bowl, stir together the water and cornstarch, then add it to the wok and stir until sauce has thickened. Add sesame oil if desired.
  15. Serve over jasmine rice and enjoy!
